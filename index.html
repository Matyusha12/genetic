Understanding Genetic Testing

Genetic testing plays a crucial role in understanding your health. It can reveal changes in your genes that may cause illness or disease.

Benefits of Genetic Testing

  • Disease Prevention
  • Personalized Medicine
  • Family Planning

Common Types of Genetic Tests

  • Diagnostic Testing
  • Predictive and Presymptomatic Testing
  • Carrier Testing
